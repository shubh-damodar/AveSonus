At Ave Sonus Consulting is one of the emerging independent consultancies in the fields of Audio Visual, Smart home automation and Acoustic Engineering.

At Ave Sonus we know that in a world of constant dynamic change, the pursuit of searching for optimal solutions that adapt to the current way of thinking is central. The solutions must provide real value, which is why at Ave Sonus we believe they have to be based on knowledge and experience.

By better understanding the physics of acoustics, the nature of the materials we work with, the science of loudspeakers and visual systems design, we are able to better develop innovative solutions, which fulfill the needs of not just today’s, but also tomorrow’s society.




IT is increasingly enveloping all fields including AV and home automation systems and we see the technologies converging and the need for home automation is becoming more necessary with the current needs.
