Dolby Atmos

Dolby® Atmos is based on the concept of object based audio technology, where any sound can exist as an independent object, to be placed and moved anywhere in your room, including overhead. Sounds will flow above and around you as they would in a real-world environment, thanks to overhead channels added to the traditional surround setup.

Dolby Atmos transports you from an ordinary moment into an extraordinary experience with captivating, multidimensional sound that fills your room and flows all around you to make your mind, body and soul like no other.
